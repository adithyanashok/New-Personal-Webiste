TubeVibe - Video Sharing App, Flutter

Project information

  • Name: TubeVibe
  • Tech Used: Flutter, Provider, Firebase
  • About App:
  • TubeVibe is a video sharing app like youtube, created with flutter and firebase
  • Features:
  • 1. Users can sign up using their email and password.
  • 2. Users can upload their own videos.
  • 3. Browse and watch videos uploaded by other users.
  • 4. Users can like videos to show their appreciation.
  • 5. Users can comment on videos to share their thoughts.
  • 6. Users can subscribe to other users' channels to stay updated with their new video uploads.
  • 7. Users can search for videos, channels, or specific content using keywords.
  • Future update:
  • 1. Notification
  • 2. Trending videos
